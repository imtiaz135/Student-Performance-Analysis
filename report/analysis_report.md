# E-commerce Sales Analysis Report

## Dataset Overview
The dataset contains 100 sales transactions with information about products,
regions, prices, quantity sold, and total revenue.

## Objectives
- Analyze sales performance
- Identify top products
- Understand regional demand
- Study monthly trends

## Key Insights
1. Laptops generate the highest total revenue.
2. March shows the highest monthly sales.
3. North region contributes the most sales.
4. West region has comparatively lower demand.

## Business Recommendations
- Increase marketing in low-performing regions.
- Focus inventory on high-revenue products.
- Investigate seasonal trends affecting sales.

## Conclusion
The analysis highlights clear sales trends and provides actionable insights
for improving revenue and regional performance.
